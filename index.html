HISTORY OF RAJPUTS

The Rajputs formed the Rajput Kingdom that controlled areas of northern India from the 7th to the 12th centuries C.E. During this period, the Rajput Kingdom helped to further spread Hinduism, Buddhism, and Jainism in the region, in addition to building beautiful architecture and publishing books on science and culture
